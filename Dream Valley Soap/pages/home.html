Dream Valley Soap makes soap; balms and creams. All our products are carefully handcrafted with quality natural oils and ingredients sourced locally.

Our Soaps are handcrafted with quality oils and lightly scented with essential oils and herbs. After a good long maturing time they are mild, nourishing and moisturising. Luxury and goodness in a decadent bar.

  • Bio-degradable; no added chemicals; SLES free.
  • Is grey water and septic tank safe.
  • Contains no animal products.
  • Is excellent for all types of skins.
  • Lathers, moisturizes and washes beautifully.

*Our soaps are handcrafted and hand cut and, as a result, may have some minor cosmetic and weight differences.*
