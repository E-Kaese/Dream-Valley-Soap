Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How do I care for natural soaps?

A: Store in a dark, dry, well ventilated place if not used. Handmade Soaps vary in colour. Brown & white spots are quite normal. When in use, do not allow to stand in water. Place in a soap dish that has drainage holes, away from direct sunlight. Ideally use immediately. Within six months to a year of purchase is recommended. Years beyond this period it is still very usable but will have lost some scent.

Q: What ingredients do you use in your soap?

A: Our traditional artisan soap is a beautiful hand cut bar made with care using only the finest ingredients. Quality oils are used in the making of our soaps:

Olive Oil:

-Known skin moisturizer
-Acts as a barrier to seal in the skin's natural moisture
-Sourced locally

Castor Oil:

-A wonder oil with many skin caring properties
-Produces smaller bubbles and gives intensity to the lather

Coconut Oil:

-A great all round skin loving oil
-Creates a silky textured soap
-Yields a creamy lather

Palm Oil:

-Yields a mild, firm, long lasting soap

Lye:

-Also known as sodium hydroxide
-Used in the 'saponification' process

Mountain Water:

-Adds to the purity of the product

Natural additives are used to enhance the soap's moisturizing, cleansing and soothing properties. These include:

-Natural Colourants (e.g. pink clay; spirulina)
-Pure Essential Oils (e.g. lavender; rosemary)
-Botanicals (e.g. dried herbs like rooibos tea and lavender)
-Exfoliates (e.g. charcoal or coffee)
